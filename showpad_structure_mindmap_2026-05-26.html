✅ Proposed — 7 channels, seller-intent structure
🚀 01 · Start Here ≤ 20 pinned
The 15 assets that cover 80% of weekly usage. Monthly review cadence.
🗺️ 02 · By Buying Stage 5 sections
Prospect → Discover → Prove → Negotiate → Expand. One stop per deal stage.
💡 03 · Solutions & Products 7–9 sections
One section per product line. One pinned "current" deck per section. Clear ownership.
⚔️ 04 · Compete & Justify 4 sections
Battlecards, Gartner, ROI calculators, win stories — everything to win the deal.
🤝 05 · Partners 3 sections
Hyperscalers, SI partners, tech partners. Links to partner portals for depth.
📚 06 · GTM Enablement (Internal) 5 sections
Onboarding, weekly enablers, sales plays, courses, ops. Not in seller main nav.
⚖️ 07 · Legal & Contracts (Internal) 4 sections
Unchanged in content. Access-controlled. Not in seller main nav.
Net result: From 21 visible channels to 5 seller-facing. From 132 sections to ~27. From department-first to intent-first. From a fragmented 235-tag taxonomy with 37% missing audience labels to a clean ~180-tag system with 100% audience coverage and full stage tagging.
